About Us

The MCA Department at KSR College of Engineering is proud to host an International Conference aimed at exploring the trends and advancements in computer applications. The conference provides a platform for researchers, industry experts, academicians, and students to come together and share insights, innovations, and ideas. Generative AI and Super Intelligence are not just technological trends but revolutions that are shaping the future of computer applications. The MCA Department at KSR College is uniquely positioned to explore these areas, fostering a culture of innovation, ethical responsibility, and collaborative research. By addressing these emerging issues, the department can contribute significantly to both academia and society.
